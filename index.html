Pablo Escobar👑

  • Cause of death:Gunshot wound to the head
  • Other names: Don Pablo (Sir Pablo) El Padrino (The Godfather) El Patrón (The Boss) Matar Pablo (Killing Pablo) The King of Cocaine The King of Crack
  • Net worth:US $30 billion (1993 estimate; equivalent to $64 billion as of 2021)
  • Political party:Liberal Alternative
  • Spouse(s):Maria Victoria Henao ​(m. 1976)​
  • Children: Sebastián Marroquín and Manuela Escobar
  • Conviction(s): Illegal drug trade, assassinations, bombing, bribery, racketeering, murder
  • Criminal penalty:Five years' imprisonment
